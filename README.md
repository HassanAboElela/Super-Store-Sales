# **Super Store Sales Analysis (Power BI)**

### **Objective**
Developed an executive-level Power BI dashboard to transform raw retail data into actionable business insights. This project analyzes two years of performance (2019-2020), tracking profitability, shipping efficiency, and customer behavior for a global Super Store.

### **Technical Implementation (Power BI Skills)**
* **Data Modeling:** Built relationships between sales, products, and geographical data for seamless filtering.
* **Advanced Visuals:** Integrated **Area Charts** for sales trends, **Bubble Maps** for regional profit distribution, and **Donut Charts** for segment analysis.
* **Time Intelligence:** Analyzed monthly growth spikes and seasonal trends across a 24-month period.

### **Key Insights**
* **Financial Performance:** Achieved a total revenue of **$1.57M** with a net profit of **$175K** from over **3,000 orders**.
* **Product Strategy:** **Phones and Chairs** are the primary revenue drivers, while **Technology** as a category leads in profit margins (30%).
* **Customer Behavior:** The **Consumer segment** is the largest market driver, accounting for **48%** of total sales volume.
* **Operational Trends:** **Cash on Delivery (COD)** is the preferred payment method ($667K), and **Standard Class** remains the most cost-effective shipping mode (912K units).
